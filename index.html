Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • Garlic & olive oil
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti until soft.
  2. Cook chopped onion, garlic, and tomatoes.
  3. Mix sauce with spaghetti.
  4. Season & serve hot.
